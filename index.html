Our Chapter

Benefits of actively participating in South Brunswick's FBLA Chapter.

Compete

Showcase your business prowess in 1 of 53 events and gain competitive exposure on the state and national levels. If you qualify, you will be given the opportunity to attend overnight conferences at select locations, such as Anaheim, California or Atlantic City, New Jersey, and achieve awards and recognition for your achievements.

Lead

You will be able to participate in multiple leadership activities while in FBLA. You will have the opportunity to participate in presentation and speaking events, work with subcommittees of various fields, and work towards achievement awards.

Fundraise

Spread awareness of prevalent issues by garnering public support and funds. You will be able to participate in the March of Dimes fundraiser and attend the March of Dimes Annual Walk.

Learn

You will be given all the resources and opportunities you need to learn about the business field and interests you want to pursue. From the subcommittees and presentations we provide to the outside business professionals we invite to our meetings, you have a lot to learn and explore at SBFBLA!

Business Achievement Awards

The Business Achievement Awards (BAA) are an opportunity for highschool students to improve their business and leadership skills, get involved in the community, and earn recognition for their activities. During the FBLA meetings, the BAA committee will give you the opportunity to get started on activities as well as help and guide you through them. There are four levels of the BAA, with each level giving you more opportunities to showcase you leadership skills and talents.

Award Levels

Level One - Contributor (Deadline: May 12th)
Level Two - Leader (Deadline: May 12th)
Level Three - Advocate (Deadline: May 12th)
Level Four - Capstone - (Deadline: May 12th)
